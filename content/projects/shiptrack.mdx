ShipTrack explores a simple loop: collect the idea, choose the next smallest useful slice, ship it, and write down what changed after real use.

The product is intentionally narrow. It should feel useful for solo builders and tiny teams that care more about progress than ceremony.

## Build notes

- Convert rough ideas into shippable slices.
- Track decisions near the work instead of in separate docs.
- Make public updates easy to write from the project history.
