FlowState is a quiet focus timer for deep work. The goal is to make it easier to start, protect, and review serious building sessions without turning productivity into another dashboard.

The first version focuses on session intent, a calm timer, and a short reflection after the work block ends. The interesting product question is whether the tool can help builders keep momentum without adding ceremony.

## Build notes

- Fast start flow for a new focus block.
- Session notes that stay short by design.
- A lightweight history that shows progress without becoming noisy.
