PromptLab is a small workspace for turning repeated AI prompts into reusable, testable workflows.

The project is built around the idea that prompts should be treated like product surfaces: versioned, reviewed, and improved with actual examples.

## Build notes

- Store prompt patterns with inputs and expected output shape.
- Compare revisions against real examples.
- Keep snippets easy to reuse across projects.
