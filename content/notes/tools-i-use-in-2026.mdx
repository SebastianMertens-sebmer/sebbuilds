The best tools are the ones that reduce the distance between noticing a problem and testing a working answer.

Right now, the stack is centered around Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind, Postgres, AI-assisted development, and a bias toward small deployable slices.

## What matters

Speed is useful only when the system stays understandable. I prefer tools that keep the product easy to change after the first version is live.
