Shipping changes the quality of your thinking. Before something is live, every product decision is still protected by imagination. After launch, the work has to survive contact with real attention.

That is why shipping is not only the final step. It is a feature of the process itself.

## The useful pressure

The deadline, the public artifact, and the first small audience all create pressure that clarifies what matters. You find the core faster when the product has to become real.
